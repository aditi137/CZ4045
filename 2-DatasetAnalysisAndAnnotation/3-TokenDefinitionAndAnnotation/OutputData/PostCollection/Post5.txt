In Python 3, you can use collections.ChainMap which groups multiple dicts or other mappings together to create a single, updateable view:
>>> from collections import ChainMap
>>> x = {'a':1, 'b': 2}
>>> y = {'b':10, 'c': 11}
>>> z = ChainMap({}, y, x)
>>> for k, v in z.items():
        print(k, '-->', v)

a --> 1
b --> 10
c --> 11
